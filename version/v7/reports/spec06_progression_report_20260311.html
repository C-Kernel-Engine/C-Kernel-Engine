What Changed by Run

r1: Introduced asset-derived infographic templates and topic-specific slot families. Result: the renderer contract worked, but topic binding drifted toward dominant templates.

r2: Reweighted midtrain toward topic edits and away from layout edits. Result: large jump in exact match and the strongest balanced run in the line.

r3: Narrow overweighting around the governance_path bullet-panel slice. Result: partial local gain, broad regression.

r4: Switched to generic slots like [slot:title], resolved by topic at render time. Result: topic-slot mismatch fixed, layout aliasing worsened.

r5: Removed redundant source tokens and strengthened layout-separation edits. Result: stat-cards recovered fully, compare-panels became strong, spectrum-band improved but remains weak.

r6: Added a narrow repair for bullet-panel governance_path on slate and sharpened contrast between spectrum-band and neighboring layouts. Result: spectrum-band is fully fixed on the probe and only three bullet-panel misses remain.
