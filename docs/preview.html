🔑 Key Concepts Documented

1. Constraint-Driven vs Specification-Driven

Why constraints are discovered, not defined. Three constraint types: Hardware, Performance, Compatibility.

2. The 4-Step Iteration Cycle

Identify → Hypothesize → Prototype → Measure. Repeat for compounding knowledge.

3. v1→v6 Evolution Timeline

Detailed learnings from each version: what to keep, what to discard, key insights.

4. The 70% Rule

Ship when 70% happy. The remaining 30% becomes foundation for v(next).
