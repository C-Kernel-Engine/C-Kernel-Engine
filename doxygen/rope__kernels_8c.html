RoPE (Rotary Position Embedding) Kernels

Applies rotary position embeddings to query and key vectors. Used by Llama, SmolLM, and most modern transformer architectures.

Math (Llama-style rotate-half): Split head_dim into two halves (0..half-1, half..head_dim-1). For each position m and index i in [0, half): x0 = x[i], x1 = x[i + half] x'[i] = x0 * cos(m * θ_i) - x1 * sin(m * θ_i) x'[i+half] = x0 * sin(m * θ_i) + x1 * cos(m * θ_i)

Where θ_i = 1 / (base^(2i/d)), typically base=10000.

Layout: x: [num_heads, num_tokens, head_dim] head-major cos_cache, sin_cache: [max_seq_len, head_dim/2] precomputed
